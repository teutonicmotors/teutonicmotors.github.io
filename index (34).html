Who Are We?

Teutonic Motors is a mobile automotive service specializing in German vehicles. We bring dealership-level tooling to your driveway or workplace.

We service BMW, Mini, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, VW, and Porsche.

Why Mobile?

Have your vehicle serviced at the comfort of your own home. Get transparent on-site work with parts and labor clearly itemized before we start.

Fully insured, clean work area, OEM or equivalent parts.
